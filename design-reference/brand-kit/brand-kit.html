03 / Logo

A geometric sentinel.

The eagle-head mark in umber. Keep clear space, never recolor to pure white on dark, never stretch or add shadow. Wordmark is Plex Sans 500, uppercase, 0.18em tracking.

04 / Components

Rules over boxes. Tone over elevation.

Hairline dividers separate content; shadows are barely there; radii are near-square. Buttons are small and confident — solid umber or ghost.
